UniHack — AI Student Knowledge Platform

A community-powered search tool for university students. Semantic vector search finds crowd-sourced solutions first; Google Gemini + DuckDuckGo kicks in automatically when nothing is found locally.

  • Pinecone vector DB with semantic embeddings (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) filtered by university & location
  • 4-model Gemini fallback chain for production resilience
  • Community report/block system: 3 reports from different users permanently blocks a bad answer
  • Verified AI answers auto-saved to the DB, continuously improving results over time

AI · Full-Stack · Deployed

Gemini Meeting Prep — AI Executive Briefing Console

An AI executive assistant that reads a user's Google Calendar and Gmail to auto-generate meeting briefings. Pulls the day's events, cross-references recent email threads with each attendee, and uses Gemini to produce concise, ready-to-read talking points before every meeting.

  • Multi-tenant architecture with per-user OAuth 2.0 (PKCE) sessions and signed JWT cookies
  • PostgreSQL-backed briefing cache to avoid redundant, costly LLM calls
  • Batched Gmail API requests for efficient attendee email-context retrieval
  • Multi-stage Docker builds and a Render Blueprint deployment for the live app
